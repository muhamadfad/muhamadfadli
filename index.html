Personal Journey

My Story

My name is Muhamad Fadli. I am a digital technology practitioner with a background in Informatics Engineering from Halu Oleo University (UHO), where I completed my studies in 2025. For me, technology is not just a field of study—it is a tool capable of creating change, efficiency, and new opportunities across various sectors of life.

My journey in the digital world began in earnest in 2022. During that time, I started exploring various aspects of digital technology, particularly web development and digital marketing. I learned how a website can evolve from a mere information page into a digital platform capable of driving business, building brand identity, and connecting organizations with their audiences on a broader scale.

In the same year, I also obtained a Junior Web Developer certification, which became one of the professional foundations in my journey in technology development. This certification strengthened my technical skills in building structured, efficient, and scalable web-based systems.

However, over time I realized that building technology is not enough from a technical perspective alone. Good technology must be able to answer real needs, solve problems, and provide added value for its users. It was from this understanding that I began to see technology not just as a product, but as a strategic solution for digital transformation.

Driven by this vision, I then founded a digital agency called AnoaDev. I established AnoaDev with the aim of becoming a digital business partner for various organizations, businesses, and institutions looking to develop their digital ecosystems. Through AnoaDev, I provide various digitalization services such as professional website development, web-based systems, desktop applications, and various technology solutions designed to support growth and efficiency in the digital era.

For me, building a digital solution is not just about sophisticated technology, but about how that technology can have a real impact on its users. Every project I work on always stems from the understanding that technology must be able to help people work more efficiently, build better systems, and open new opportunities for business and organizational development.

I believe the world is moving toward an era where digitalization becomes the main foundation in various sectors. Organizations that can adapt to technology will have an advantage in facing changing times. Therefore, through AnoaDev I am committed to continuing to deliver modern, strategic, and sustainable digital solutions.

This journey continues to evolve. For me, every system built, every website developed, and every digital solution created is part of an effort to drive broader and more impactful digital transformation for the future.

— Muhamad Fadli, S.T.
